Frequently Asked Questions

What services does HOSSOT Solutions offer?

HOSSOT Solutions offers comprehensive corporate compliance, audit services, HR management, and healthcare research solutions. We specialize in helping organizations navigate complex regulatory landscapes while maintaining operational excellence.

How quickly can you respond to urgent compliance issues?

We understand that compliance issues can be time-sensitive. Our team typically responds to urgent matters within 4-6 hours during business days, and we offer emergency support for critical situations.

Do you work with organizations outside of India?

While we specialize in Indian corporate compliance and regulations, we also work with international organizations that have operations in India or need to comply with Indian regulatory requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing varies based on the scope and complexity of services required. We offer both project-based and retainer-based arrangements. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.
